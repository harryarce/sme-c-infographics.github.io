Get started

From zero to
"hello world" in four steps.

A consistent onboarding path whether you're adding SMS, email, calling or chat.

Create a resource

Provision an Azure Communication Services resource in the Azure portal or via ARM/Bicep.

Choose a channel

Select voice, video, chat, SMS, email or advanced messaging for your scenario.

Install the SDK

Add the .NET, JavaScript, Java, Python, iOS, Android or PowerShell SDK to your app.

Authenticate & send

Issue an identity token or use a connection string, then send your first message or make your first call.

Email at scale.

Send transactional and bulk email from your own verified domain — the recommended path for workloads throttled by Exchange Online TERRL / RRL limits.

  • Custom domains with SPF, DKIM and ARC
  • Delivery analytics, bounce & suppression lists
  • EU Data Boundary supported
