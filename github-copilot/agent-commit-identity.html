What this doesn't say

  • Third-party doesn't mean worse. GitHub-hosted Claude/Codex can keep review workflows in GitHub, and custom Apps can give teams more control.
  • Copilot has its own gaps. The content-exclusion limitation above is real and should be called out in sensitive repos.
  • Identity is an audit question. Before rollout, confirm the actual bot/App actor in your organization and test it against branch rules.

About this infographic

A platform-engineering view of commit-author identity for AI coding agents around GitHub. The claim is narrow: hosted-vs-standalone identity changes audit and setup work, but does not make an agent inherently safer or more capable.
