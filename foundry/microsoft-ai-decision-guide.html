Cost Considerations

Pricing Model Overview

How each platform is priced — from per-user licensing to pay-as-you-go consumption.

M365 Copilot

  • Per-user/month subscription
  • Requires M365 E3/E5 or Business Premium
  • Predictable, fixed cost per seat
  • No additional compute charges

Copilot Studio

  • Per-tenant monthly license
  • Includes message capacity allotment
  • Pay-as-you-go for overages
  • Can be included in M365 license

Azure OpenAI

  • Pay-per-token (input + output)
  • Provisioned throughput option
  • Pricing varies by model tier
  • No upfront commitment required

Microsoft Foundry

  • No platform fee (pay for services used)
  • Compute costs for training/inference
  • 1,800+ model marketplace pricing
  • Azure Savings Plans available

Foundry Tools

  • Pay-per-API-call or per-unit
  • Free tiers for development
  • Volume discounts at scale
  • Commitment tier pricing available
